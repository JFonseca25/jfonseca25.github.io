J. N. Bastos Fonseca

  • Currently, I am a PhD student at the Socially Intelligent Artificial Systems (SIAS) Group and the Prosocial Dynamics lab, at the University of Amsterdam. My supervisor is Prof. Fernando P. Santos.
  • More information can be found on my GitHub profile, and on my LinkedIn page. You can also reach me via j.n.bastosfonseca [at] uva.nl.
